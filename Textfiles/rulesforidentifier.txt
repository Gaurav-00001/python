RULES FOR IDENTIFIERS:-
(1) They can be combination of upper and lowercase letters, digits or an underscore. 
(2) Cannot start with the digit. 
(3) We cannot use special symbols like !,#,@,%,$,etc in our identifier.
(4) It can be of any length.

Example - 
=>correct :- myvariable, my_variable, my_variable1, My_variable123.
=>incorrect :- my variable , 1myvariable , my@variable.
