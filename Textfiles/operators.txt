Operators:- Symbols to perform different operations between two operands or more than two operands.
Types of operators:-
(1) Arithmetic Operators:- + , -, *, /, %, **.
(2) Relational/Comparison Operators:- ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=.
(3) Assignment Operators:- =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=.
(4) Logical Operators:- not, and, or.
